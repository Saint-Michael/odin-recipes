Lasagna

Description

Here is a recipe for some easy and delicious lasagna that you can enjoy tonight.

But first we will need to gather the required ingredients and we will be on our way.

Ingredients

  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 1 pound of ground beef
  • 1 onion, diced
  • kosher salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 1 can of diced tomatoes
  • 1 tablespoon of italian seasoning
  • 1 package of whole milk ricotta
  • 3.5 cups mozzarella
  • 1 large egg, beaten
  • 2oz grated parmesean
  • 2 tablespoons chopped parsley

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Lightly oil a 9 by 13 baking dish or coat with nonstick spray.
  2. In a large pot of boiling salted water, cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  3. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium high heat. Add ground beef and onion and cook until beef has browned, about 3-5 minutes, making sure to crumble the beef as it cooks; season with salt and pepper, to taste. Drain excess fat. Stir in tomatoes and Italian seasoning until well combined.
  4. In a medium bowl, combine ricotta, 1/2 cup mozzarella and egg; set aside.
  5. Spread 1 cup tomato mixture onto the bottom of a 9 by 13 baking dish; top with 3 lasagna noodles, 1/2 of the ricotta cheese mixture and 1 cup mozzarella cheese. Repeat with a second layer. Top with remaining noodles, tomato mixture, 1 cup mozzarella cheese and Parmesan.*
  6. Place into oven and bake for 35-45 minutes, or until bubbling. Then broil for 2-3 minutes, or until top is browned in spots.
  7. Let cool 15 minutes. Serve, garnished with parsley, if desired.
